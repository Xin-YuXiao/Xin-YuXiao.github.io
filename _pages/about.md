I'm a master student from [School of Astronomy and Space Science], [University of Chinese Academy of Sciences]([https://www.ucas.ac.cn/]).
My research interest includes Astronomical Geodynamics, Astronomy and Human-Computer Interaction.

I majored in New Media Technology in [Qilu University of Technology(Shandong Academy of Sciences)]([https://www.qlu.edu.cn/]) for my undergraduate degree. Due to my interest in Astronomy, I switched to Astrometry and Astromechanics for my postgraduate study. 

I am currently receiving training in the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences and the National Astronomical Observatories, and I am engaged in cross-research of computer and astronomy. Goal is to develop more tools for astronomers solve difficulties in astronomy.
